Contacting Apple

Apple Store

You can purchase apple products and accessories from Apple and other manufacturers by calling (800) MY–APPLE (800–692–7753), 7 days a week from 7:00 a.m. to 11:00 p.m. Central time.
You can get information about an order you placed by phone at (800) 692–7753, 7 days a week from 7:00 a.m. to 11:00 p.m. Central time.

How to Buy for Business

If you are a business or professional user, call 1–800–854–3680, 7 days a week from 7:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. Central time.
Corporate and Government Sales:

  • Apple Enterprise Sales (877) 412–7753
  • Apple Government Sales (877) 418–2573

How to Buy for Education

If you are a student or teacher, visit the Apple Store for Education or call 1–800–692–7753, 7 days a week from 7:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Central time.
If you are buying on behalf of an educational institution, visit the Apple Store for Education Institutions or call 1–800–800–2775, 7 days a week from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. Central time.
